# Privacy Policy for PII Redactor for Gemini

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

## Overview
PII Redactor for Gemini is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed to process your information locally in your browser.

## Information Collection
- **No Personal Data Collection:** This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personally identifiable information (PII), keywords, custom rules, or usage statistics to the creator or any third party.
- **Local Processing:** All redaction, keyword matching, and LLM processing occur entirely within your local browser environment.
- **Local Storage:** Your settings and custom rules are stored locally on your device using the browser's storage API and are never uploaded to the cloud by this extension.

## Data Usage
- The information you input into Gemini is processed locally to redact sensitive data.
- Redacted information is only temporarily held in memory to "rehydrate" responses within your browser session.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support page.
